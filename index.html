Octo

What started as an experiment in the capabilities of Progressive Web Apps (PWA) is now a fully functioning Markdown writing app built specifically with developers in mind. The boundaries between native applications and web applications is getter smaller by the day, and Octo is an investment in the future of these technologies.

For more information about Octo, you can check it out on GitHub, follow its dedicated Twitter account, or use the app at octo.app.
